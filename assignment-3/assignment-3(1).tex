\section*{Question}
A box has 8 red balls and 8 green balls . Two balls are drawn randomly in succession from the box without replacement. The probability that the first ball drawn is red and the second ball drawn is green is \\
A) $4/15$   \hfill (C) $7/16$ \\
(B) $1/2$     \hfill   (D)  $8/15$ \\
\section*{Solution}
Given, 2 balls are drawn without replacement in quick succession\\
Let A be the event of getting red ball on the first draw\\
and B be the event of getting green ball on the second draw\\
\begin{align}
 \mbox Pr(A)=  \frac{\mbox {no of red balls in box} }{\mbox{ total no of balls in box} }\\
\mbox Pr(A)=\frac{8}{16}
 \end{align}
 \begin{center}
 The probability of drawing green ball after it is given that the first ball drawn is red = \mbox Pr(B$|$A)\\
 \end{center}
 \begin{align}
\mbox Pr(B|A)= \frac{\mbox {no of green balls in box} }{\mbox{ total no of remaining  balls in box} }\\
\mbox Pr(B|A)=\frac{8}{15}
 \end{align} 
 \begin{center}
  the probability that the first ball is red and second ball drawn is green is  = Pr(A$\cap B$)\\
 
 \end{center}
 Pr(A$\cap B$) =  Pr(B$|$A)\times Pr(A)\\\\
\mbox Pr(A\cap B)= $(8/15)\times(8/16)=(4/15)$
